Siamese Cat

The Siamese cat is one of the first distinctly recognized breeds of Asian cats. Known for their sleek bodies and striking blue eyes.

Maine Coon

The Maine Coon is a large and sociable cat, sometimes referred to as "the gentle giant." They have a distinctive ruff of fur around their neck.
